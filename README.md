# Ethical Use of AI

## Overview
-- This overview highlights the key principles and challenges surrounding the ethical use of AI, including data privacy, bias prevention, transparency, accountability, and the responsible design and deployment of intelligent systems. It emphasizes the importance of aligning AI technologies with human values, ensuring they support—not replace—human judgment, and establishing guidelines that protect users and communities. By examining real-world examples and best practices, this presentation aims to equip audiences with a clear understanding of how ethical considerations shape the future of AI and why they are essential for building trust and safeguarding society.
