Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly transforming the way we work, communicate, and make decisions—and with this power comes an equally important responsibility. As AI tools become more integrated into daily life, understanding their ethical use is essential to ensure fairness, transparency, privacy, and accountability. This presentation explores how we can harness the benefits of AI while minimizing risks, promoting human-centered values, and ensuring that technology serves society in a responsible and trustworthy manner.
